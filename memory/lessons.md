## Entry Log

> Format: one entry per line. Routines **append** — never rewrite or delete. weekly_review may prune entries older than 90 days.
>
> `YYYY-MM-DD | routine | tag | observation`
>
> Tags:
> - `mistake`    — something went wrong the agent should avoid repeating
> - `win`        — something worked better than expected and is worth repeating
> - `macro`      — market-environment observation (VIX, trend, Fed, etc.)
> - `execution`  — how the order/fill behaved (slippage, gap, partial fill)
> - `thesis`     — whether the fundamental catalyst played out as expected
>
> Keep each observation ≤ 160 chars. State the fact; don't editorialize.
